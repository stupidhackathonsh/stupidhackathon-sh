STUPID SHIT NO ONE NEEDS & TERRIBLE IDEAS HACKATHON SHANGHAI

The first stupid Hackathon Shanghai was held 3PM - 1AM, April 21st-22nd, 2017, at New York University Shanghai.

ABOUT:

A one-day event where participants conceptualise and create projects that have no value whatsoever.

Organised by Zhang Zhan, Nicholas Sanchez, David Santiano, Richard Lewei Huang, Saphya Monet Council, Jack B. Du, and Katarzyna Olszewska.
